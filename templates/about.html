ABOUT
RAYS OF GRACE JUNIOR SCHOOL

OUR HISTORY

Rays of Grace Junior School is a vibrant and nurturing educational institution proudly registered under the Ministry of Education and Sports. Our journey began in the year 2015, initially operating as Mountain of Olives Primary School. Through dedication and growth, we transitioned to our current name, Rays of Grace Junior School in the year 2018,

establishing ourselves from the outset as a comprehensive learning environment for both boys and girls, offering both day and boarding facilities. From our humble beginnings in Kirugu village, Wakisi Division, Njeru Municipality, Rays of Grace Junior School was founded with a vision to provide excellent education to all kinds of children and prepare servant leaders for the next generation.
