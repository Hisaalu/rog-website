Our Vision

To build a community of faith in Uganda by providing an excellent education to all kinds of children, supporting missionaries and preparing servant leaders for the next generation.

Our Mission

To provide a rigorous and balanced quality education that challenges students to excel academically and live out a Biblical worldview.

Core Values

Courage, Honesty, Empowerment, Respect, Integrity, Spiritual Growth, Hope

Our Programs

Discover our comprehensive educational programs designed to nurture young minds and develop well-rounded individuals through academic excellence and extracurricular activities.

Academics

Our comprehensive academic program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.

Music

Our music program introduces students to the joy of musical expression through singing, instrument playing, and music theory.

Soccer

Our soccer program promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Students learn fundamental skills and the importance of fair play.

Computer Studies

This program introduces students to digital literacy, basic programming, and technology skills essential for the 21st century.
